The Conqueror

Story

 

In the year of Our Lord 886, the Vikings settled in the British Isles.

A treaty with the King of Wessex granted them lands, called the Danelaw.

Despite the treaty, the Danes kept raiding Angles, Saxons and Celts territories.

The weakness of the throne pushed local Angles and Saxons lords to fight even among themselves, besides quarrelling with the common Norman enemy.

The Celts kept seeking booty in the lands that were once theirs. Some saw it as an opportunity to regain them.

All the British Isles lay before anyone who would try to seize the power to unite them once and for all.

Will be a new king for all Britain? Who will he be?
